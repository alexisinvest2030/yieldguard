FAQ

Common questions.

Does YieldGuard need access to my private keys?▾
No. YieldGuard is read-only. It only needs your public wallet address. We never ask for private keys, seed phrases, or signing permissions of any kind.
How does the Telegram alert work?▾
You start a chat with our Telegram bot and link it to your account in under 60 seconds. When a threshold is triggered, you receive a formatted message immediately — no app needed.
Which chains are supported?▾
Pool Explorer covers 76+ chains via DeFiLlama. Active backend monitoring covers Solana, Ethereum, Base, Arbitrum, and Optimism across all 10 supported protocols.
How accurate are the APY figures?▾
We pull directly from each protocol's official API — Kamino REST, Morpho GraphQL, and Aave's subgraph. The data is the same as what you see on each protocol's own frontend.
Can I pay with crypto?▾
Yes. We accept USDC on Arbitrum, Base, Solana, BNB Chain, Polygon, and Ethereum via NOWPayments. You can also pay with any major card via Stripe.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?▾
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of the billing period.
